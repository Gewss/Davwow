What is DAVWOW?

Davao and WOW

DAVWOW came from the word DUHVOW. Since before Davao named as it is, before one of the tribes who lived into it called it as Duhvow. From that history we came up from the name Davwow. And instead of naming our enterprise as Duhvow we change 'vow' into 'wow' since are main goal is to offer a good and high quality products and service to meet the satisfaction of our customers.
